About Me

Dr. Jiangmin Ding is a postdoctoral researcher at Pusan National University. His research focuses on how AI-enabled innovation, digital transformation, and organizational capabilities enhance resilience and sustainability in global supply chains.

He has published and reviewed for international journals and welcomes collaborations in supply chain management, sustainability, and digital business innovation.
